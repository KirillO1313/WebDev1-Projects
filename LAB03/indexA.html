About The Circus

At The Emotional Support Circus, we believe that every creature deserves an occasional standing ovation. Whether they hang from the ceiling, nap in sunbeams, or juggle in oversized shoes. Our mission is to rescue, rehabilitate, train in emotional support, and rehome all kind of crearures focusing on bats, cats, and clowns, giving each the care, attention, and animal balloons they need to thrive.

Where to Find Us

The Emotional Support Circus is a fully mobile menagerie of smiles, squeaks, and light chaos on wheels. As a traveling circus, we roam from town to town setting up wherever a little laughter and a lot of face paint can make a difference. Our latest location is always posted below- we update it as often as a clown honks their horn.

Get Involved

Want to lend a helping (or juggling) hand? The Emotional Support Circus thrives on community support to keep the tents standing, the clowns smiling, the cats purring, and the bats happily squeaking. You can find the link to our donation page below- every contribution keeps the magic in motion. We’re also always looking to expand our volunteer team with folks dedicated to making sure our critters get all the care and attention they deserve. Check out our team portal for current opportunities — no matter your skill set, there’s almost certainly room for you under our tent.
